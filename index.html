Why Programming?

Programming has always been an interest of mine ever since my Dad became a programmer in 2011. It was something that, to this day, he really passionate about, and my interest in it developed through that exposure. I've always enjoyed problem solving and the challenge that programming brought to me, and no matter what I was working on, I found appreciation in getting one step closer to the finished product, no matter how small that step was. Programming lit a fire inme that I never knew I had, and it only makes me want to pursue it more.
